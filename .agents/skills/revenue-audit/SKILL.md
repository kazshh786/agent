\# REVENUE AUDIT SKILL

When invoked, follow this 4-step diagnostic:

1\. \*\*Friction Analysis:\*\* Locate the Primary CTA. Is it "above the fold"? Is the booking/contact flow more than 3 clicks?

2\. \*\*Psychological Leakage:\*\* Assess if the copy uses "Loss Aversion" or "Social Proof." If not, identify where the trust gap is.

3\. \*\*Structural Audit:\*\* Analyze page load speed and mobile-responsiveness as potential conversion killers.

4\. \*\*Recovery Pitch:\*\* Draft a 3-point recovery plan. Start with "The Quick Win" (immediate change), "The Structural Fix" (layout change), and "The Authority Move" (content/SEO strategy).
