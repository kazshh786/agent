Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking, gear integration, and crew support at KS Studio.

What’s the typical timeline from booking to shoot?

For standard bookings, you can secure your slots instantly in our real-time diary once you complete the brief qualification wizard. Set builds or complex custom gear rigs require a quick 15-minute alignment call with our production lead at least 48 hours prior to load-in.

Are lighting and grip gear really included in the packages?

Yes. Our rates are structured as complete value stacks to eliminate hidden checkout fees. The Editorial Stack includes our Profoto lighting suite, and the Commercial Stack buyout includes full overhead Arri SkyPanel grids rigged as standard. Basic grip stands and sandbags are always free of charge.

What happens if we run over our booked session times?

We frame our spaces in full-day buyouts (9 hours) and half-day blocks (4 hours) to avoid clock-watching anxiety. In the event of a critical campaign delay, overtime can be requested at a flat rate of £250/hour, subject to next-day booking availability.

Do you support custom set constructions?

Absolutely. Studio Alpha features direct high-clearance shutter doors for vehicle loading and scenic flat deliveries. The Residency Stack includes dedicated setup crew hours to support your set builders during load-in days.
