\# GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM

\- \*\*Grid:\*\* Strict 8px base unit grid.

\- \*\*Typography:\*\* Major Third scale (1.250). Use clean, high-readability sans-serifs.

\- \*\*Color Ratio:\*\* 60-30-10 rule (Primary, Secondary, Accent).

\- \*\*Motion:\*\* All animations must use `ease-out-quad` timing functions; max duration 600ms.

\- \*\*Interactive Elements:\*\* Minimum touch target size 48px x 48px.

\- \*\*Visual Balance:\*\* Prioritize asymmetrical balance and generous negative space to establish a "luxury" aesthetic. No claustrophobic layouts.
