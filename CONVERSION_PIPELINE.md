\# SALES \& PITCHING PIPELINE

\- \*\*The Yes-Ladder:\*\* Always begin by confirming small facts with the prospect ("You want to reduce phone calls, correct?") before asking for the project.

\- \*\*Loss Aversion:\*\* In every pitch, highlight the revenue "bleeding" from their current site (e.g., "Every lead that bounces because your mobile site is slow is effectively £X lost per month").

\- \*\*Pricing Strategy:\*\* Do not use "Hourly Rates." Frame all pricing as "Value Stacks." Justify costs by linking features directly to the expected conversion lift.
