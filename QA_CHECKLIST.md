\# PRE-FLIGHT QA PROTOCOL

Before notifying the Architect (Kasim) that a site is ready:

1\. \*\*Performance Check:\*\* Simulate Lighthouse audit. Ensure all page assets are optimized (WebP/AVIF format).

2\. \*\*SEO Integrity:\*\* Verify all pages have unique Meta Titles, Descriptions, and OpenGraph tags for WhatsApp/Social sharing.

3\. \*\*Accessibility Audit:\*\* Scan for WCAG 2.1 contrast ratios and ARIA label implementation on all interactive elements.

4\. \*\*Link Health:\*\* Verify 100% of internal links are absolute relative paths and functional.

5\. \*\*Responsive Test:\*\* Validate layout at 375px, 768px, and 1440px breakpoints.
