"Continuous UX optimization and conversion protection contracts."

What is a Conversion Protection Retainer, and how does it secure bookings?

A Conversion Protection Retainer is a continuous optimization contract designed to monitor and improve your site's conversion rate over time. Instead of simple software updates, we run ongoing A/B testing, remove user flow friction, and maintain loading speeds to protect your B2B sales pipeline.

Why are hourly marketing and design contracts a conflict of interest?

Hourly billing structures reward agencies for working slower. Our value-backed growth retainers are built on flat-rate pricing tiers that align our incentives directly with your business goals, guaranteeing that every engineering change focuses strictly on increasing checkout or booking margins.

How do we guarantee constant layout speed and accessibility compliance?

We execute weekly performance diagnostics, run Cloudflare edge caching checks, and perform manual WCAG 2.1 contrast audits. This keeps your digital presence operating under 0.8 seconds on mobile devices while remaining fully accessible and protected against data leaks.
