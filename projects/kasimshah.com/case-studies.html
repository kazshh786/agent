Vertical: Aesthetics & Private Medical

Elite Aesthetics Clinic Rebrand

"Rebuilding checkouts and scheduling pipelines to stop mobile lead leakage."

The Challenge: The clinic was spending £2,500/month on search advertising but suffered a massive 64% mobile bounce rate. Their page loaded in 3.8 seconds on a standard 4G connection, and scheduling an appointment required navigating a complex 6-field contact form with no calendar visibility.

The Solution: We scraped their legacy WordPress setup and engineered a lightweight native page on a strict 8px layout grid. We replaced the generic form with a 2-click Material Design calendar interface connected directly to their live booking API.

The Results:

+147% Mobile Bookings
0.6s Page Load Speed
+42% Booking Margins

"We were wasting half of our local advertising spend. Kasim’s native checkout engine and booking flow doubled our weekly appointments without increasing our ad budget."

— Clinical Director
Vertical: Premium Home Trades

High-Ticket Installation Qualifying

"Using interactive vetting and automated qualification to filter and secure high-intent leads."

The Challenge: The trade services company faced the opposite problem: they had traffic and phone calls, but over 70% of submissions were low-value tire-kickers who didn't qualify. This clogged up their administration team, costing them hours in manual vetting calls.

The Solution: We built an asymmetric landing page featuring an interactive 4-question vetting checklist. If a prospect checked the right variables, they were routed to an instant appointment scheduling card; otherwise, they were automatically sent to a downloadable resource library.

The Results:

-35% Cost-Per-Lead (CPL)
92% Qualification Rate
+183 New Accounts Q1

"Our administrative overhead dropped by a third overnight, and the leads we receive now are pre-qualified and ready to buy."

— Operations Manager
