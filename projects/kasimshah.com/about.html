Enterprise Pedigree

The Founder's Story

"Why I brought multi-million pound conversion engineering to local B2B businesses."

Before founding this independent practice, I spent years working in the high-stakes world of enterprise user experience. At AO.com—one of the UK’s largest ecommerce retailers—and Zuto—a major car finance broker—my day-to-day focus was simple: optimize conversion pathways.

My background represents a rare **cross-disciplinary fluency**—bridging the gap between front-end visual psychology, secure database route engineering, and real-time API integrations. In enterprise environments, a 0.1% increase in mobile checkout efficiency translates directly into millions of pounds. I was trained to look at websites not as static brochures, but as **high-precision software engines** that either guide a user seamlessly to a transaction or bleed revenue at every step.

The Leaky Bucket Reality

During my enterprise tenure, I noticed a frustrating pattern. High-value local businesses—medical clinics, private tutors, customized manufacturing firms, and high-ticket trades—were paying thousands of pounds to search marketing agencies to drive traffic to their sites.

But when that traffic arrived, it was dumped into slow, bloated WordPress templates that were completely broken on mobile. Over 60% of their hard-earned visitors bounced instantly because booking a slot took 8 clicks instead of 2.

These businesses were pouring water into a leaky bucket, actively funding their competitors. They didn't need more "theoretical marketing talks." They needed conversion engineering.

The Kasim Shah Standard

I launched my practice with a single, non-negotiable rule: Zero Templates.

Every layout we deploy is coded natively, built on a strict 8px grid system, and utilizes Material Design 3 interactive components. This guarantees load times under 0.8 seconds and strict WCAG accessibility compliance.

We don't sell hourly rates, and we don't hide behind vanity metrics like impressions or clicks. We stack value, protect your margins, and align our work directly with your bookings.
